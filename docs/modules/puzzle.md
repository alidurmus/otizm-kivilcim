- **Primary Voice:** Josh (eğlenceli, oyun yönergeleri)

- **Drag & Drop:** Smooth puzzle piece movement

- **Difficulty Levels:** 4, 9, 16 piece options
- **Touch Targets:** Large pieces for easy manipulation
